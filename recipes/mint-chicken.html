Mint Chicken

Description

A favorite family recipe that my Mom has made for hundreds of guests. Fresh lemon juice and mint leaves are a must! Serve over hot cooked white rice.

Ingredients

  • 2 skinless, boneless chicken breast halves-cut into bite sized pieces.
  • 1 clove garlic, crushed
  • half a cup of all-purpose flour
  • half a cup of margarine
  • a quarter of a cup of fresh lemon juice
  • half a cup of chopped fresh mint leaves

Steps

  1. In a medium bowl, mix together the chicken pieces and garlic.
  2. Coat with flour and shake off excess
  3. Heat the margarine in a large skillet over medium-high heat. Add the chicken pieces; cook and stir until browned
  4. Add the mint leaves and lemon juice, cover and steam for about 5 minutes, just until the chicken is cooked through. Do not over cook the chicken or it will be tough.
